Upgrade:

v1.0 to v1.1

There have been quite a few changes and optimizations in the newsletter send process. One of the more important changes was made to the way (x)html templates are generated and archived. If you have modified your newsletter_template_html.htm template and the style sheet to suit, I recommend that you not replace your newsletter_template_html.htm template -or- the pnstyle/style.css file, but rather compare the files and migrate changes into your modified newsletter_template_html.htm template. Basically, it's a full (x)html template and as such requires a doctype and some basic meta tags.

The way archive viewing has changed as well, since we're now using full (x)html documents we can display them as a single document "outside" of our site's template. There are advantages in handling the previews in this manner: no css conflicts with the local theme template, potentially improved SEO and better aesthetics. The ability to flush archives has been added to the main Newsletter administration screen. It is recommended that you flush your archives to take advantage of this new feature.
